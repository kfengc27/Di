Trivial facts about Toronto, Canada

The coldest temperature ever recorded in Toronto was -31.3°C on January 4, 1981. Toronto is North America’s third largest venue for movie production. There are over 25,000 jobs in feature film production. Toronto is 76.5 meters above sea level. The highest point – 209 meters – is at the intersection of Steeles Avenue West and Keele Street.
