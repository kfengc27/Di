Trivial facts about Atlanta, U.S.

Atlanta is the only city in North America to have been destroyed as an act of war when General Sherman burnt it to the ground in November 1864. Atlanta is home to 71 streets with a variation of the word “Peachtree” in their names: Peachtree Avenue, Peachtree Plaza, etc. Coca-Cola was first invented in downtown Atlanta on May 8th, 1886.
